Meet The Crew

Pat McAfee

Host

Patrick McAfee is a former All-Pro NFL punter, stand up comedian, football and professional wrestling analyst, and a social media star. Pat began his sports career after earning a scholarship to kick footballs at West Virginia University. Drafted by the Indianapolis Colts in the seventh round of the 2009 NFL Draft, he played in two Pro Bowls during his eight years in the NFL.

Since retiring from professional football, McAfee has been a frequent fixture in sports media, guesting for Fox Sports' college and NFL broadcasts in late 2018 before joining ESPN's Thursday Night College Football team in July 2019. Pat also provides commentary of WWE's Friday Night Smackdown on Fox & select pay-per-view events.

In fall 2019 Pat became the host of a new weekday sports talk show called The Pat McAfee Show which features a video simulcast on YouTube. Known for his candid, relatable, and refreshing opinions, Pat's insight, dynamic storytelling and robust sense of humor helped him build a huge and loyal fan base with more than 2.5 million social media followers. In 2019, Pat took his act on the road launching a stand up comedy tour, playing to sold-out crowds at every venue.

Pat and his father Tim operate The Pat McAfee Foundation, a non-profit organization which provides scholarships to the children of US Military personnel. Learn more at The Pat McAfee Foundation.
